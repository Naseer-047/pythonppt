MindCare AI

Mental Health Chatbot Web Application

Introduction

Mental health is a vital aspect of student life. Academic pressure, emotional stress, and personal challenges affect well-being. MindCare AI offers a friendly, AI-powered platform for support.

Objectives

  • Develop a real-world Python web application
  • Integrate AI chatbot interaction
  • Create responsive and modern UI
  • Enhance mental well-being using technology

Applications & Impact

Helps students manage stress and anxiety
Promotes early emotional support
Can be integrated into college systems

Conclusion

MindCare AI demonstrates how Python, AI, and web technologies can solve real-world problems through innovation and empathy.
